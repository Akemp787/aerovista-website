NAFI Membership Data Modernization Support

AeroVista supported a large-scale migration from Tovuti to Member365 involving hundreds of files and datasets for more than 16,000 members. The engagement focused on multi-source reconciliation, data quality improvement, validation workflows, import-ready datasets, executive reporting, and operational support during migration.

30,000+Records in a single source file
16,000+Member profiles
Multiple SystemsReconciled and consolidated
  • Migrated data from Tovuti to Member365.
  • Cleaned, standardized, and validated membership records.
  • Matched records across Tovuti, Stripe, MemberClicks, and related sources.
  • Validated memberships, subscriptions, transactions, payments, courses, and engagement data.
  • Prepared import-ready datasets, reporting outputs, and launch support materials.
  • Supported final migration review and post-launch validation workflows.
