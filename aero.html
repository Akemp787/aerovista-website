Purpose

Built to strengthen the consulting work.

Aero is not the product clients buy. Clients hire AeroVista for consulting judgment, analysis, reconciliation, reporting, and implementation support. Aero is being developed as an internal workspace to help make that work more structured, traceable, and repeatable.

The goal is simple: reduce spreadsheet chaos, document assumptions clearly, connect findings back to source data, and produce evidence leaders can review with confidence.

How Aero Helps

Investigation workflows for messy operational data.

Aero is designed around the kind of work that often happens before a migration, dashboard, board report, or automation project can be trusted.

Developing capabilities

  • Profile complex datasets across multiple source systems
  • Track validation rules, assumptions, exceptions, and review notes
  • Reconcile member, payment, subscription, course, and transaction data
  • Identify inconsistencies, gaps, duplicates, and logic conflicts
  • Produce clearer workpapers and executive-ready summaries

Consulting First

Aero supports the engagement. It does not replace the consultant.

Evidence Review

Connect findings to source files, business rules, and documented assumptions instead of relying on screenshots or undocumented spreadsheet steps.

Reconciliation Logic

Support repeatable checks for active/lapsed status, payment history, duplicate records, subscription dates, and migration readiness.

Executive Summaries

Translate technical findings into concise summaries leaders can use to make decisions, approve next steps, or reduce project risk.
