Privacy Policy

AeroVista Analytics collects the information you choose to provide when you contact the company, request a consultation, or discuss a potential project. This may include your name, email address, company, project details, timeline, and service needs.

The information you send is used to respond to your inquiry, evaluate project fit, prepare consultation notes, and communicate about requested services. AeroVista Analytics does not sell your contact information.

Website inquiry messages are processed by the AeroVista Analytics backend and delivered through a secure server-side email provider. Delivery errors are logged without exposing private message contents to website visitors.

Inquiry records may be retained for business follow-up, troubleshooting, and security review. Access is limited to AeroVista Analytics business purposes.

Email is used for initial inquiries. Please avoid sending sensitive credentials, regulated data, or confidential datasets until a project scope and appropriate data-handling process are in place.

To ask about your information or request an update, email contact@aerovistaanalytics.com.
