Andrew Kemp

Founder of AeroVista Analytics

I am Andrew Kemp, founder of AeroVista Analytics. I help organizations turn messy operational data into trusted business intelligence through analytics strategy, executive reporting, Power BI and dashboard development, data migration, data reconciliation, reporting automation, and decision support.

My experience includes aviation, higher education, nonprofit and membership associations, local government, and business analytics. I work directly with executive leadership and project teams to build analytics solutions, reporting systems, migration workflows, automation, and business intelligence deliverables that connect technical detail with practical business decisions.
