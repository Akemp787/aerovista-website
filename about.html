Andrew Kemp

Founder of AeroVista Analytics

Andrew Kemp founded AeroVista Analytics to help organizations turn messy operational data into trusted business intelligence. His work spans analytics strategy, executive reporting, Power BI and dashboard development, data migration, data reconciliation, reporting automation, and decision support.

Andrew is pursuing an M.S. in Business Analytics at Georgetown University and holds a Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics from The George Washington University. He also earned a bachelor's degree with dual majors in Economics and Spanish, a concentration in International Economics, and a minor in Business Spanish.

His experience includes aviation, higher education, nonprofit and membership associations, local government, and business analytics. He has worked directly with executive leadership and project teams to build analytics solutions, reporting systems, migration workflows, automation, and business intelligence deliverables that connect technical detail with practical business decisions.
