Founder

Your Data. My Priority.

I’m Andrew Kemp, founder of AeroVista Analytics. I help organizations move beyond simply knowing what happened to understanding why it happened, what it means, and what they should do next. By cleaning and connecting complex data, investigating the drivers behind performance, and improving reporting and automation, I turn fragmented information into clear, practical insights that support confident decision-making. I also help businesses apply the latest advances in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automation to reduce manual work, uncover deeper insights, and build more efficient, reliable operations.

My background includes financial analysis, business intelligence, data migration, process automation, and operational reporting, with experience supporting organizations across aviation, higher education, local government, professional and membership associations, and other mission-driven sectors. I am pursuing an M.S. in Business Analytics at Georgetown University and hold a Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics from The George Washington University. I also earned a bachelor’s degree with dual majors in Economics and Spanish, a concentration in International Economics, and a minor in Business Spanish. This combination of analytical, business, technical, and communication experience allows me to understand both the numbers and the broader context behind them—so I can explain the why, introduce modern capabilities where they create real value, and help organizations make smarter decisions.
